Solar Tracker (programmed in Java)

  • In this project I worked within a group of 4 people in order to construct a device that rotates a solar panel towards the sun in order for it to be of maximum effectiveness. I focused on programming the arduino to control the motor which rotated the system. I did this by reading the change in voltage caused by the photoresistors as they were exposed to more or less light. I then programmed the motor to rotate accordingly in the direction which was determined to be of greater sunlight.
